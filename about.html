WHO WE ARE

We are a group of creative minds,passionate about IT and sports, with a mission to change the world? We begin from our little sphere of influence.... "Cricket"

Our Values

Snicket is based on the premise that sporting informations in our locality should be readily available for anyone and everyone at anytime. This we believe would help Sports Stakeholders in Nigeria and Africa at large, to make informed critical decisions. To guide us on the journey to enhance transparency and thereby eliminate veiled corrupt practices that characterise Sports in our Society.


MISSION: Our mission is to change the outlook of cricket in Nigeria vis' a vis Africa. we believe that the first step to enhancing the sporting experience in Nigeria is to establish an extensive online presence and community comparable to that which can be found anywhere in the world.
