IBEJU LEKKI CRICKET CLUB


Ibeju Lekki cricket club was founded in 2004 by Mr. Owolabi Oduguwa and Mr. Bayo Balogun. The club played in the second division of the Club Cricket Committee League Lagos (CCC league) in its first year, ending the season as runners-up. The next season 2004/2005, the Team was crowned champions of the second division with an unbeaten run for the whole season and gained promotion to the top division. The canaries have since then been the most successful cricket club in the country winning over 20 trophies including 8 cccleague championship and 4 national club championship.
