JOSHUA AYANNAIKE


Josh is a right hand opening batsman and wicket keeper. A current member of the Nigeria Senior Menâ€™s National Team. Who has also participated in various under age national developmental team u-17 in 2008 and U-19 in 2010. He started his club career as a schoolboy in IMCC 2009 and joined Ibeju Lekki feeder Team in 2010. With the ILCC2 team promoted to the division 1 of the CCCLEAGUE, he was one of the outstanding players and became vice captain in 2011. He won the young player of the year award and got promoted to the first team in 2012 as Ibeju 2 relegated to the second division of the CCCLEAGUE. He got called up to the National Team the following year as he helped the Lagos State Cricket Team win a Gold medal at the EKO 2012 NSF and played his debut match for the yellow greens in 2013 against Vanuatu as Nigeria won the ICC World Cricket League Div 7 in Botswana. Josh Ayannaike also has a degree in Political Science from the University of Lagos.
